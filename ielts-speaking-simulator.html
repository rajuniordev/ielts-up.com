IELTS speaking test simulator

You are going to have an online IELTS speaking test.

  1. Press "Start speaking test" and answer the first question aloud.
  2. After pressing "Start speaking test" you can use "Show transcript" option.
  3. When you are ready to move on, click "Ask me next question".
  4. After you finish, you can
    • hear all your speaking test recording by pressing "Play my speaking test";
    • download the recording by pressing "Download my speaking test".
    • send any of your recordings for assessment by clicking "Upload my recording for correction" and uploading your file.
Good luck!

To be able to play and download your speaking recording, use Mozilla Firefox, Opera or Google Chrome browser!
