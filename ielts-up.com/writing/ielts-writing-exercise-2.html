IELTS Writing exercise #2. Level: band 5

Correct the mistakes:

  1. Do you have a dog? Yes, I have.

    Do you have a dog? Yes, I .


  2. I love my city, because it is too beautiful.

    I love my city, because it is beautiful.


  3. What you can do in such situation?

    What in such situation?


  4. There isn't a lot of people here.

    There a lot of people here.


  5. Do you like to go somewhere tomorrow?

    you like to go somewhere tomorrow?


  6. I am thinking of to go home, because I am very exhausted.

    I am thinking of home, because I am very exhausted.


  7. This jacket is more cheap than the other one.

    This jacket is than the other one.


  8. I love that show, because is funny!

    I love that show, because funny!


  9. Before make a dinner, you should practice a lot.

    Before a dinner, you should practice a lot.


  10. My parents always told me a bedtime history, when I was little.

    My parents always told me a bedtime , when I was little.
