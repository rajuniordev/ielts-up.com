How does it work?

  1. Choose your Writing question (you can use IELTS Writing questions list for IELTS task 2 essays).
  2. Make a secure payment with your credit card on our website. You can pay in any currency, just check the exhange rates.
  3. Send us your writing(s) in a MS Word document or text file by e-mail to writing@ielts-up.com. Please, also attach your receipt.
  4. The examiner will send you a correction within 24-48 hours.
 

What is included

The corrected version will include:

  • Final band with explanation
  • Four marking criteria scores
